About BKINE

Bach Khoa Industrial Equipments JSC

Established in 2010, BKINE is one of Vietnam's leading fire protection solution providers specializing in UL/FM fire pump systems, clean agent fire suppression systems, inert gas fire suppression systems, high pressure water mist systems, and fire & gas detection systems.

With extensive experience in industrial, power generation, oil & gas, logistics, semiconductor, infrastructure, and commercial projects, BKINE delivers reliable fire protection solutions in compliance with international standards including NFPA, FM Global, UL, and ISO.
